    URC 2019

    This year University Rover Challenge was organized from 1 June 2017 to 3June 2017. For this year's competition 82 teams registered. 36 of them were called for the finals at Mars Station, Utah. ARES Robotics went into the competition with great excitement. Our rover performed all four tasks to the best of its capabalitites.
    We achieved 8th position all over the world.
    Our rover was the best performer from India and Asia.
    Our biggest highlight was the score of 100 out of 100 in Science Cache Task.
